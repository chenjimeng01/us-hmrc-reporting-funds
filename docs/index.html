Offshore funds that have successfully applied to HMRC for reporting fund status receive favourable UK tax treatment: gains on disposal are taxed as capital gains rather than as offshore income gains at income-tax rates. This matters particularly for US-connected investors resident in the UK, since US-domiciled funds and ETFs on this list can be held tax-efficiently on both sides of the Atlantic.

Always confirm a fund’s status against the official HMRC list before acting, and check the “with effect from” and “ceased” dates for the period you held the fund. This page is an informational extract, not advice.
